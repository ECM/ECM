Publications

About

Since ECM13, ECM has published selected conference papers in special issues (e.g. [1][2]) of the Journal of Marine Science and Engineering (JMSE)[3].

For ECM1 through ECM12[4] conference papers were published in the proceedings of the International Conference on Estuarine and Coastal Modeling, American Society of Civil Engineers (ASCE), Coasts, Oceans, Ports, and Rivers Institute (COPRI).

These collections contain peer-reviewed papers on estuarine and coastal modeling with a focus on model development, testing, application, skill assessment, interpretation, and visualization of results
