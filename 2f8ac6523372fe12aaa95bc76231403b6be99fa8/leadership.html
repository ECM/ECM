Past Leadership

Conference Chairs:

1989-2011: Dr. Malcolm Spaulding

2013-2021: Dr. Richard P. Signell

Malcolm L. Spaulding, then Assistant Professor and now Emeritus Professor, Ocean Engineering, University of RI initiated the conference series and served as chair of the organizing committee and sole or lead editor of the conference proceedings (ECM1 to ECM12). He was ably assisted by a team of leading professionals in the field serving in the roles of co-editor of the proceedings and members of the organizing and advisory committees in the field, drawn from academia, government, and private industry. The names of members of the committees are provided in the meetings section. The leadership of the conference was assumed by Rich Signell, Oceanographer, US Geological Survey starting in ECM13 and continuing till 2021.
