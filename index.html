Welcome to the Estuarine and Coastal Modeling (ECM) Conference Site!

Since 1989, the Estuarine and Coastal Modeling Conference (ECM) has provided a venue for commercial, academic, and government scientists and engineers from around the world to present and discuss the latest results and techniques in applied estuarine and coastal modeling, with ample opportunity for networking.


Starting in 2021, we have joined forces with the Coastal Estuarine Research Foundation (CERF), as the ECM conferences will be held as part of the CERF Biennial Conference! The virtual format for the ECM16/CERF 2021 26th Biennial Conference was certainly different, but it created the opportunity to reach a more expansive group of scientists and students from around the world to address the science and management of coastal ecosystems and communities. We plan to return to an in-person format for ECM17/CERF 2023 27th Biennial Conference, hosted in Portland, Oregon! We hope to see you there!
